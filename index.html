Sugar Skull Crystal Game

Can you match the computer's score?

At the start of each game, the computer generates a random number between 21 and 120. In addition, each sugar skull crystal has a random number assigned it between 1 and 12 at the start of each game. No skull has a duplicate number. Watch your score to see if you can click on the right combination of skulls to add up and match the computer's number.
